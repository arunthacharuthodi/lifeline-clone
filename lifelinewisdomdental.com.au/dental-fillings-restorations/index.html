What is Dental Filling?

Our dentists properly examine your oral health and if they feel that a tooth requires immediately treatment for cavity to remove decay then dental filling may be used to fill the decayed area. Composite or indirect fillings are used to fill the decayed portion. Sometimes combine process are used to fill a decayed area.

Filling Materials

Gold

Silver Amalgam

Ceramics/Porcelain

Glass Ionomer

Inlays/Onlays

BENEFITS OF DENTAL FILLINGS

  • Prevent the cavity growth
  • Improves the structure of the tooth
  • Reinforces cracked teeth
  • Tooth fillings can develop the color of your teeth
  • Filling can guard your teeth from decay

YOUR APPOINTMENT INCLUDES:

  • Treatment Plan

    Get a treatment plan with full assessment of oral health

  • Scanning of Teeth

    The doctors recommend the different views XRays to determine the cavity

  • Selection of Filling

    You can select the desired filling such as gold, silver or porcelain

  • Anesthesia for Removing Cavity

    To eliminate the pain our doctor will numb the area with local anesthesia and complete the process

  • Direct or Indirect Filling

    While filling the cavity doctors can use inlay or onlay fillings to fill the area properly to prevent the diseases to attack again

  • Schedule the Next Visit

    You will be given a schedule to visit for checkup. Our doctors will checkup the oral health on each visit and monitor the progress of treatment.

TIPS FOR DENTAL FILLING

  • Food and Drinks after Filling

    Be restrained to hot meals, coffee, tea, sticky items, solid and chewy

  • Maintain the Oral Hygiene

    Maintain the oral hygiene after meal and use brush and floss

  • Sensitivity

    You may feel sensitivity right after filling for few days to weeks.

  • Teeth Brush

    Use appropriate brush and paste to clean the teeth after every meal.

  • Feel Discomfort

    Contact immediately If you feel discomfort or having any other serious problem
